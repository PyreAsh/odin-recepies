Pictured: Inferior Japanese Ramen

This is how we prepare my world famous ramen the Japanese are super jealous of.

  1. First we start by boiling a pan of water about pinky depth.
  2. Next we submerge the frozen Ramen noodles (yes frozen) in the boiling water.
  3. We shall wait 30 minutes for the water and Ramen noodles to combine to form a white sludge
  4. Finally, we will pour it into a bowl and enjoy.
